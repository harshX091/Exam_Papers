📷 Have a physical paper? Scan it first — it's free & fast
▼

Use one of these free apps on your smartphone to scan your paper before uploading. They auto-detect edges, fix perspective, and produce clean small PDFs.

🟡 Google Drive BEST
Already on your Android phone — no download needed. Tap + → Scan. Needs Google account.
Android iPhone
🔴 Adobe Scan
Free Adobe account required. Excellent quality, edge detection, OCR text recognition.
Android iPhone
📋 Tips for a good scan
  1. Good lighting — scan near a window or under a bright light. Avoid shadows.
  2. Flat surface — lay the paper flat on a table, don't hold it in the air.
  3. Hold phone directly above — look straight down at the paper, not at an angle.
  4. Export as PDF — not as a photo/image — to keep file size small.
