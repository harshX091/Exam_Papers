📷 Have a physical paper? Scan it first — it's free & fast
▼

Use one of these free apps to scan your physical paper, save it as a PDF, then upload it here.

🔴 Adobe Scan BEST
⬇ Android ⬇ iPhone
📋 Step-by-step
  1. Open Adobe Scan and scan your paper.
  2. Tap Save PDF.
  3. Tap ⋮ (3 dots, top right).
  4. Tap Copy to device — PDF is saved to your phone.
  5. Come back here and upload the saved PDF using the form below.
🟡 Google Drive
⬇ Android ⬇ iPhone
📋 Step-by-step
  1. Open Google Drive app on your phone.
  2. Tap the ＋ button (bottom right corner).
  3. Tap Scan from the menu that appears.
  4. Scan all your pages, then tap Upload — file saves to Drive automatically.
  5. Find the file in Drive → tap ⋮ (3 dots) → Download.
  6. Come back here and upload the downloaded PDF using the form below.
💡 Tips for a clear scan
  1. Good lighting — scan near a window or bright light. Avoid shadows on the paper.
  2. Flat surface — lay paper flat on a table, don't hold it in the air.
  3. Hold phone straight above — look directly down, not at an angle.
  4. Save as PDF — not as a photo/image — file size will be much smaller.
